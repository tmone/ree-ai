# District-specific insights for Ho Chi Minh City

## Quận 1 (District 1)
**Character:** CBD, business hub, high-density urban
**Price level:** Very High (100-200 triệu/m²)

**Key areas:**
- Bến Nghé: Financial center, offices
- Bến Thành: Shopping, tourism
- Nguyễn Thái Bình: Expat apartments

**Amenities:**
- Shopping: Vincom, Saigon Centre
- International schools: Few (mostly in Q2/Q7)
- Hospitals: FV Hospital, Columbia Asia

**Best for:**
- Young professionals
- Business owners
- Investors (rental yield)

**Search radius modifiers:**
- Near office: 500m
- Near shopping: 1km

---

## Quận 2 (District 2) / Thủ Đức City
**Character:** Expat hub, international schools, modern compounds
**Price level:** High (50-100 triệu/m²)

**Key areas:**
- **Thảo Điền:** Luxury villas, expat community, riverside
- **An Phú:** International schools (AIS, BIS), compound living
- **Cát Lái:** Affordable, developing area
- **Thủ Thiêm:** New urban area, modern high-rises

**Amenities:**
- International schools: AIS, BIS, ISHCMC
- Shopping: The Vista Verde, Masteri Thảo Điền
- Metro: Line 1 (Ben Thanh - Suoi Tien)

**Best for:**
- Expat families
- Families with school-age children
- Luxury villa buyers

**Search radius modifiers:**
- Near international school: 2km
- Near metro: 1km

---

## Quận 7 (District 7)
**Character:** Master-planned, family-friendly, modern infrastructure
**Price level:** High (50-100 triệu/m²)

**Key areas:**
- **Phú Mỹ Hưng:** Gated compounds, international schools, complete amenities
- **Tân Phú:** Affordable apartments
- **Tân Quy:** Mix of old and new

**Amenities:**
- International schools: SSIS, Renaissance, ILA
- Shopping: Crescent Mall, SC VivoCity
- Parks: Starlight Bridge, Central Park

**Best for:**
- Vietnamese families with children
- Expats (PMH area)
- Investors (stable appreciation)

**Search radius modifiers:**
- Within PMH: 3km radius is acceptable
- Near international school: 2km

## Thủ Đức (Thu Duc City - Merged Q2, Q9, Thu Duc)
**Character:** New administrative center, rapid development, investment hotspot
**Price level:** Medium to High (30-80 triệu/m²)

**Key areas:**
- **Khu Đô Thị Vạn Phúc City:** Modern, complete amenities
- **Khu Metro:** Near Line 1 metro stations
- **Vincity Grand Park:** Mega development, 271ha

**Amenities:**
- Metro Line 1: Operational
- Universities: ĐHQG (VNU), FPT, RMIT
- Tech parks: Saigon Hi-Tech Park

**Best for:**
- Young buyers (affordable)
- Investors (growth potential)
- Students (near universities)

**Investment keywords:**
- "Tiềm năng tăng giá" (growth potential)
- "Hạ tầng mới" (new infrastructure)
- "Metro" (transportation)

## Location-Based Query Expansion Rules

### When user says "gần trường quốc tế" (near international schools)
**Auto-suggest districts:** Quận 2, Quận 7, Quận 1
**Radius:** 2km from schools
**Query expansion terms:** international school, AIS, BIS, ISHCMC, SSIS, VAS, Renaissance, British School, Australian School, IB School
**Schools to consider:**
- AIS (An Phu - Q2)
- BIS (Thao Dien - Q2)
- SSIS (Phu My Hung - Q7)
- ISHCMC (Thao Dien - Q2)
- VAS (An Phu - Q2)
- Renaissance (Phu My Hung - Q7)

### When user says "gần metro" (near metro)
**Districts:** Thủ Đức, Bình Thạnh, Q1
**Radius:** 1km from stations
**Stations:**
- Ben Thanh (Q1)
- Van Thanh (Binh Thanh)
- Thu Duc stations (Thu Duc)

### When user says "khu yên tĩnh" (quiet area)
**Districts:** Q7 (inside PMH), Q2 (An Phu compounds)
**Filters:**
- Gated community
- Small alley (< 5m)
- Low traffic

### When user says "tiện đi làm quận 1" (convenient for D1 work)
**Districts:** Q1, Binh Thanh, Q3, Q10
**Commute time:** <= 30 minutes
**Transportation:** Metro, main roads

### When user says "đầu tư sinh lời" (investment for rental)
**Districts:** Thủ Đức (growth), Binh Thanh (rental demand), Q7 (stable)
**Property types:** Apartments (easy to rent)
**Near:** Universities, offices, metro

## District Comparison Patterns

### Expat-friendly areas
**Priority order:** Q2 (Thao Dien) > Q7 (PMH) > Binh Thanh (VHCP)
**Key factors:** International schools, Western amenities, English-friendly

### Family with children (Vietnamese)
**Priority order:** Q7 (PMH) > Q2 (An Phu) > Thu Duc (Vincity)
**Key factors:** Schools, safety, parks, playgrounds

### Young professionals (budget-conscious)
**Priority order:** Binh Thanh > Thu Duc > Tan Binh
**Key factors:** Price, commute time, nightlife, cafes

### Luxury buyers
**Priority order:** Q2 (Thao Dien villas) > Q7 (PMH villas) > Q1 (penthouses)
**Key factors:** Exclusivity, prestige, amenities
